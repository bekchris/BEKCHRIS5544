Tribute Page

The tribute page project is a project where users can pay homage to individuals who have made significant contributions to various fields. I created personalized webpages dedicated to these individuals, highlighting their achievements and impact. Through this project,I gain hands-on experience in HTML and CSS, honing my web development skills. By constructing a visually appealing and informative tribute page, I showcased my ability to structure content, apply styling, and effectively communicate my subject's importance. The tribute page project serves as a valuable stepping stone in the journey of becoming proficient web developer while celebrating the legacies of inspiring figures.
